## Description: <br>
Polanyi Skill provides a Michael Polanyi-style thinking aid for analyzing tacit knowledge transfer, skill acquisition, apprenticeship, SOP execution gaps, AI/tool substitution boundaries, and science-philosophy questions. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[enzyme2013](https://clawhub.ai/user/enzyme2013) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Learners, educators, researchers, engineering leaders, and knowledge-management practitioners use this skill to reason through tacit knowledge, expertise transfer, documentation limits, apprenticeship design, and related philosophical questions from a Polanyi-inspired perspective. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may over-trust the first-person Polanyi persona or treat extrapolated answers as documented historical views. <br>
Mitigation: The skill instructs agents to distinguish documented Polanyi views from extrapolation, especially for modern topics after 1976, and to avoid inventing exact quotations, page numbers, letters, or interview content. <br>
